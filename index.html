1. About This App

Ve ung dung nay

Learn Vietnamese (Hoc Tieng Viet) is a free educational application developed by Le Phap Media ("we", "us", "our"). The app is designed to help foreigners learn the Vietnamese language through vocabulary, grammar, conversations, quizzes, and cultural content.

Developer: Le Phap Media

2. Information We Collect

Thong tin chung toi thu thap

A. Information You Provide:

  • Display name: Used only for personalization within the app

B. Information Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Learning progress and quiz scores
  • Study streak and XP points
  • Completed lessons history
  • App settings (voice speed, pitch)
This data is stored using SharedPreferences and NEVER leaves your device. We do NOT have access to this data.

C. Information Collected by Third Parties:

  • Google AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (GAID/IDFA), IP address, and general device information for the purpose of serving advertisements. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Firebase Remote Config may collect basic device and app information to deliver configuration settings. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Quang cao (Google AdMob)

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob to support free access to all educational content.

Types of ads displayed:

  • Banner ads (bottom of screens)
  • Interstitial ads (between lessons)
  • App Open ads (when opening the app)

Data collected by Google AdMob:

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information:

  • How Google uses your data
  • AdMob data disclosure
Ads require an internet connection to load. All learning content remains available offline, but ads will not display without internet.

5. Internet & Offline Usage

Internet & Su dung ngoai tuyen

Internet is OPTIONAL.

WITHOUT internet (100% offline):

  • All lessons, vocabulary, grammar, conversations
  • Quizzes, flashcards, games
  • Text-to-Speech pronunciation
  • Learning progress tracking

REQUIRES internet:

  • Loading advertisements (AdMob)

The app is fully functional without internet. Ads simply will not display when offline.

8. GDPR & CCPA Rights

Quyen GDPR & CCPA

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) under GDPR and California residents under CCPA:

Your rights include:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the app (Progress screen)
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete all data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Right to Data Portability: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can disable internet to prevent any data transmission

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, most data protection concerns relate to third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase). Please refer to Google's privacy controls.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR Article 6):

  • Legitimate interest: Serving ads to support free app access
  • Consent: Ad personalization requires user opt-in

9. Data Security

Bao mat du lieu
  • All learning data is stored in your device's private app storage (SharedPreferences)
  • This storage is sandboxed by the Android operating system
  • No other apps can access your learning data
  • We do NOT operate any servers that store user data
  • Communication with Google services (AdMob, Firebase) is encrypted via HTTPS

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

10. Data Retention & Deletion

Luu tru & Xoa du lieu

Local data retention:

  • Data is retained on your device until you delete it
  • No data is backed up to any cloud service by this app

How to delete your data:

  • In-app: Settings → Reset All Progress
  • System: Android Settings → Apps → Learn Vietnamese → Clear Data
  • Complete removal: Uninstall the app

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

Third-party data retention: Google may retain advertising-related data according to their retention policies. To manage Google data: Google Data & Privacy

11. Text-to-Speech

Phat am
